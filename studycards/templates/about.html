About Study Cards

Study Cards are based on the idea of index cards. For a single subject you would have a set of cards perhaps wrapped in a rubber band. Unlike hand written cards, you can easily change what information appears on each side of study cards. You can review your cards with all of the information easily viewable on one side, or flash your cards by putting some items on the front and the rest on the back.

To begin either create a new Card Set or Select a Card Set for more options.
