ABOUT STUDY CARDS

{% endblock titlebar %} {% block content %}

Welcome to Study Cards, the virtual study helper.

Study Cards are based on the idea of index cards. With these virtual cards you get to choose how they are organized. And each time you use them they can be organized a completely different way. Unlike hand written cards, you can easily change what information appears on each side of study cards. You can review your cards with all of the information easily viewable on one side, or flash your cards by putting some items on the front and the rest on the back.

General Instructions

Home Page: When you start the program you begin on the home page. From here you can do one of three actions.

Select a Card Set: Use the drop down menu to select one of the Card Sets you have already created. Then clikc the Select Cardset button.

Create a new Card Set: If this is your fist time using the program or you are ready to create a new Card Set simply click the Create CardSet button. Then fill in the title and description. The title must be unique.

Edit a Card Set: Use the drop down menu to select one of the Card Sets you have already created and then click the Edit Cardset button to pull up a form that will aloow you to edit the title and description of that cardset.

Card Menu: Once you have selected a cardset you will be taken to the Card Menu. Here you can select one of a variaty of options.

Add New Card The first thing you will need to do is add new cards to your Card Set. Click on the Add New Card button to open a form that will allow you to enter the infromation including up to 2 keywords, a text entry, and a picture for each card. In the Add New Card Form you can click on Add Another Card. This will save the Card you are working on and clear the form to add an aditional Card. When you enter your last Card click on the Finish button to return to the Card Menu.

Reivew Cards: To review your cards with all of the information on one side simply clikc on the Reivew Cards button. This will allow you to view each card in your set in the order they were added.

Edit/Delete Cards: Once you are reviewing your cards an Edit and Delete button will appear in the menu bar at the bottom. Use these bottons to Edit or Delete the card that is currently on the screen.

Flash Cards: To use your cards as Flash Cards click on the Flash Cards button. This will open up a form that allows you to select which items show up on the front of the card, and the back of the card. This is the true power behind StudyCards because you can create multiple different versions of your cards each time you run the Flash Card part of the program.

Items only on the back will appear as ? on the front of the card so you can remember what it is your are trying to guess. Items from the front of the card will appear gray when the back of the card is revealed.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Create a new Set of Cards:

  1. Go to the home page localhost:5000/

  2. Click on "Create New Cardset"

  3. Enter the Title and Description of the new Cardset

  4. Click on "Create New Cardset"

Select a Cardset:

  1. Go to the home page localhost:5000/

  2. Use the drop down menu to select a Cardset

  3. Click on "Select Cardset

Edit a Cardset:

  1. Go to the home page localhost:5000/

  2. Use the drop down menu to select a Cardset

  3. Click on "Edit CardSet"

Add Cards to Cardset:

  1. Follow the instructions above for Selecting a Cardset

  2. Click on "Add a New Card"

  3. Enter the Key1, Key2, and Definition of the new Card.

  4. Click "Add Another Card" to add that card and then create a new card.

  5. Click "Finish" to add final Card and return to Card Menu.

Review Cards

  1. Follow the instructions above for Selecting a Cardset

  2. Click on "Review Cards"

  3. Use the "Previous Card" and "Next Card" to Clycle through the Cards
    Note: The card number you are viewing is displayed to the top right of the Card.

Edit/Delet Cards

  1. Follow the instructions above for Review Cards

  2. Select the Card you want to edit or delete

  3. Click on "Edit"

  4. Click on "Finish" after you have made changes to save those changes

  5. Clock on "Delete" to delete the current card.

Flash Cards

  1. Follow the instructions above for Selecting a Cardset

  2. Click on "Flash Cards"

  3. Use the form to select which items will appear on the Front of the Card and then the Back.
    Note: You can select items to appear on both front and back, or neither front or back.

  4. Click on "Submit" The cards will be sorted in a random order and the Front of the first card will appear.

  5. Click on "Back" to reveal the back of the card.

  6. Click on "Next" to reveal the front of the next card.
